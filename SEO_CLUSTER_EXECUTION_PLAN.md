## 5. Internal Linking System

Internal linking should become a rule-based system, not a manual afterthought.

### Required link pattern for every pillar

Each pillar should contain:

- links to every relevant supporting post in its cluster
- at least one link to its primary commercial page
- at least one link to a related comparison or conversion page

### Required link pattern for every supporting post

Each supporting post should contain:

- 1 link back to its pillar
- 2 links to sibling posts in the same cluster
- 1 commercial link

### Commercial link mapping

Use this default mapping:

- practical workflow/tutorial posts -> `/pricing`
- comparison posts -> `/compare`
- founder/business strategy posts -> `/company`
- voice differentiation posts -> `/compare`
- onboarding/setup posts -> `/pricing`

### Anchor text rules

Use descriptive anchors, not vague language.

Preferred examples:

- `Google Workspace automation guide`
- `AI agent vs chatbot comparison`
- `custom AI solutions for business`
- `voice commands for Google Workspace`
- `Naurra pricing and plans`

Avoid:

- `click here`
- `learn more`
- repeated exact-match anchors everywhere

### Minimum internal linking target

For long posts:
- **4-6 contextual internal links**

For standard supporting posts:
- **3-4 contextual internal links**
